Perfect Boiled Eggs

Home

Description:

This is my go-to method for flawless hard-boiled eggs. A splash of vinegar and a pinch of salt help the shells come off effortlessly, with no mess or tearing. I've been using this trick for years!

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Vinegar
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps:

  1. Bring the salt, vinegar, and water to a boil.
  2. Gently boil the eggs for about 14 minutes.
  3. Transfer the eggs to ice water.
  4. Once cooled, peel the eggs.
