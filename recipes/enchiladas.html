Enchiladas

Home

Description:

I love the combination of jalapeño cream cheese and shredded chicken, so I started experimenting with those ingredients. That's how this jalapeño cream cheese chicken enchiladas recipe was born—and everyone who tries it absolutely loves it!

Ingredients:

  • 3 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
  • 3 ½ teaspoons garlic powder, divided
  • 1 ½ teaspoons cayenne pepper, divided
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 large onion, minced
  • 2 jalapeño peppers, seeded and minced
  • 1 (8 oz) package cream cheese, cut into large cubes
  • ½ teaspoon paprika
  • ½ teaspoon chili powder
  • ½ teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 (28 oz) can green enchilada sauce
  • 7 flour tortillas
  • 8 oz shredded Monterey Jack cheese, divided

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Season chicken breasts with ½ tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp cayenne, salt, and pepper. Place in a baking dish.
  3. Bake for 30-35 minutes until fully cooked. Let cool and shred with two forks. Set aside.
  4. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion and jalapeños and cook until onion is translucent, about 5 minutes.
  5. Add cream cheese and stir until melted.
  6. Add remaining garlic powder, cayenne, paprika, chili powder, and cumin. Mix well.
  7. Stir in shredded chicken and remove from heat.
  8. Pour half of the enchilada sauce into a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  9. Lay tortillas out, add a line of chicken filling, and sprinkle each with about 1 tbsp of cheese. Roll up tortillas.
  10. Place rolled tortillas seam-side down in the dish.
  11. Pour remaining sauce over the top and sprinkle with the rest of the cheese.
  12. Bake for 30 minutes or until bubbly and cheese is melted.
